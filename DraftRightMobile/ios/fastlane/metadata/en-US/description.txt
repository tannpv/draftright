DraftRight rewrites your text in any tone, anywhere on your iPhone.

Type in Mail, iMessage, Slack, Notes, or any app. Select the text, tap Share, pick DraftRight, choose a tone. Your text is rewritten and copied to your clipboard. Paste it back.

EIGHT TONES

Simple, Natural, Polished, Concise, Technical, Claude, Grammar Check, Translate.

WORKS WITH YOUR LANGUAGE

Vietnamese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Hindi, and dozens more. Type with your iPhone's existing input method — DraftRight handles the rewrite.

PRIVACY

Your text is processed by the AI provider you choose and not stored on our servers. We don't train models on your input. We don't sell your data. Full policy at draftright.info/privacy.

CROSS-PLATFORM

Available on iPhone, iPad, macOS, Windows, Linux, and the web. One account works everywhere.

PRICING

Free: 20 rewrites per day. Pro: $4.99/month unlimited. Manage at draftright.info/pricing.
